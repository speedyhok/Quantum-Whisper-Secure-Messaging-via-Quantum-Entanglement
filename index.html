No-Cloning Theorem

A quantum state cannot be copied. If Eve attempts to read Adam's qubit q2, she destroys the entanglement in the channel. This is not encryption protected by math complexity — it is protected by the physical laws of nature.

Why 2 Classical Bits?

Teleportation is not faster than light. Mahi's qubit contains the state, but rotated. He requires Adam's 2 measurement bits (c0, c1) to know which correction gates (X, Z) to apply. Without them, Mahi's qubit is pure random noise.

What Fidelity Means

Fidelity measures how close Mahi's state is to Adam's: 1.0 means an exact replica was successfully teleported. A value of 0.5 represents random noise, which is the immediate signature of an active eavesdropper.
